WHO WE ARE

We at the social welfare and development committee, readily work with a motto to empower society and transform lives to bring out the positive changes in the society. We aim to be a helping hand towards everyone in society by organizing various events and activities throughout the year. Main strength of our committee lies in equality and unity amongst every member. We are always ready to do someone a good turn to be good for society with no expectation in return.

OUR PROMISE

At SW&D, we promise to create a safe-space where people, who share our objective, can express themselves freely and work together as a team. We promise to work with vigor and diligence to ameliorate society through a myriad of activities. We also vow to utilize our platform to inculcate social values and helpful nature amongst people and move one step closer to achieving our ultimate goal-sustainable development.

OUR MISSION

To formulate, implement, and coordinate policies and programs for the needs and welfare of poor and needy people, as well as to provide quality social services to help them in the face of challenges in life. We promote social and family responsibilities in order to develop individual potential, empower, and build a team that is innovative, cohesive, and professional.

AATMABODH

Aatmabodh is the initiative taken by SW&D for the betterment of society. It is a flagship event of our college. Among the kingpins are the elderly and homemakers. Senior citizens and homemakers learn basic skills from students at our college. Pandemics could not dampen the enthusiasm of students. The committee members made last year's event a huge success by conducting it online with a high level of dedication and willingness to do whatever it takes.



आत्मबोध हा SW&D कमिटी मार्फत वर्षानुवर्षे चालत आलेला एक वार्षिक उपक्रम आहे. यात कॉलेजचे विद्यार्थी घरोघरी जाऊन जेष्ठ नागरिक व लहान मुलांना आवश्यक असणाऱ्या प्राथमिक गोष्टी शिकवतात. आत्मबोध हा आपल्या कॉलेजचा एक प्रमुख उपक्रम असून यात सहभागी होणाऱ्या विद्यार्थ्यांची संख्या जास्त आहे. मागील वर्षी कोरोनामुळे या उपक्रमाला स्थगिती येईल असे वाटत असताना सर्व कमिटी मेंबर्सनी निर्धार करून ऑनलाइन आत्मबोध पार पाडून दाखविला.
