WHO
 WE ARE

We passionately believe in sustainable social change. There is a 100% chance that the world would be a better place if everyone helps another individual.The volunteers and co-ordinators of our activities belong to the age group 18-22. These students of VIT Pune hold a higher ground of morals and values.

OUR MISSION

Donate something more valuable than money; TIME. Each student dedicates 30 hours towards welfare of the society. We ensure awareness in people, educate them, help them overcome their gadget fears. Spending time in orphanages & old age homes are moments cherished indefinitely! Cleaning buses, monuments and hills, planting seeds & rain water harvesting is a small 'give back' to nature.
