About The Event

"Mi Atrey Boltoy" is a State-Level Marathi Speech Competition organized for everyone to put forth their views and opinions on concerning topics.

Where

The event will be held virtually.

When

Round 1: 25th February
Final round: 27th February

FAQs

  • What is mode of conduct for this event?

    The event will be held virtually.

  • What are the languages for elocution?

    This event is held only in Marathi as a tribute to Great Speaker P.K. Atre.

  • What are the criteria to participate in this event?

    This event open for all the students of any colleges and schools.

  • Where can we register?

    Click here
