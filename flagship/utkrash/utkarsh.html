About The Event

Children are the future of tomorrow. Kindling proper education, values and life skills in these little ones will sow seeds for a better nation. Do the underprivileged toddlers get this opportunity? This program is thus to comprehend and eliminate the educational inequality in primary and secondary schools. Students visit schools and catered towards the development of children and reached to the depths of their minds. The main motto of the activity is to teach them playfully. Teaching sessions on soft skills, science projects, career counselling are conducted by volunteers to open up the students so that they can express their thoughts well. Teaching and guiding innocent souls was enjoyed by all.
